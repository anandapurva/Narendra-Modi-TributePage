Poem written by Mr. Narendra Damodar Das Modi:

Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga

Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Saungandh mujhe iss mitti ki, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga

Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Meri dharti mujhse puch rhi, kab mera karj chukaoge

Mera ambar mujhse puch rha, kab apna farj nibhaoge...

Maine vachan diya Bharat maa ko , tera shish nhi jhukne dunga

Saungandh mujhe iss mitti ki, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga ... Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga...

Wo lut rhe h mere sapno ko, mai chain se kaise so jau..

Wo bech rhe h Bharat ko, khamosh kaise ho jau

Ha maine kasam uthayi hai, ha maine kasam uthayi hai..

Mai desh nhi bikne dunga, Saungandh mujhe iss mitti ki, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga... Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Wo jitne andhere layenge , mai utna ujala launga

Wo jitni raat badhayenge, mai utna suraj uganunga

Is jal fareb ki aandhi me, mai deep nhi bujhne dunga...

Saungandh mujhe iss mitti ki, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Wo chahte hai jage na koi, bas raat ka karobaar chale

Wo nasha bandte jaaye, aur desh yuhi bimar chale

Par jaag rha h desh mera, par jaag rha h desh mera...

Har Bharat vaasi jitega, Saungandh mujhe iss mitti ki, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga... Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Maa bahno ki kismat par , jidd najar lagaye baithe hai

Har insaan hai yha dara dara, dil mai khauf jamaye baithe hai..

Mai apne desh ki dharti par, ab dard nhi ugne dunga

Mai desh nhi rukne dunga, Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga Mai Desh nhi Mitne Dunga, Mai Desh nhi Jhukne Dunga

Ab ghadi faisle ki aayi, hamne hai kasam ab khayi

Hame fir se dohrana hai, aur khud ko yaad dilana hai

Na bhatkenge na atkenge, kuch bhi ho is bar , ham desh nhi mitne denge...!!!
